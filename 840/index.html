Together with composer Christian Drew, Alex curates 840, a concert series dedicated to presenting new experimental music for chamber ensembles. Since its launch in 2015, the series has featured music by 78 composers, premiering works by Laurence Crane, John Lely, Georgia Rodgers, Leo Chadburn, Lawrence Dunn and Sergei Zagny amongs many others. 840 has been generously supported by the Arts Council England, the Richard Thomas Foundation, the Hinrichsen Foundation and the RVW Trust.

Visit eightforty.co.uk for more information.
