Arbonauts - Biped's Monitor

Biped’s Monitor is a site-based performance merging physical and aerial theatre with an operatic score. Inspired by Italo Calvino’s magical folktale The Baron In the Trees and created for the disused chapel and trees of the Nunhead Cemetery, Biped’s Monitor sold-out in both 2012 and 2013.
